Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 17, 2025

Welcome to Trend Fit App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our app.

1. Data We Collect

Trend Fit App accesses the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Workout Data (e.g., active calories, heart rate, workout duration)
  • Personal Health Data (e.g., age, gender, height, weight)

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your HealthKit data outside your device.

In addition, the app stores basic preferences (e.g., unit settings) and debugging logs locally on your device. These do not contain HealthKit data and are not shared.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data solely for displaying and analyzing workout trends to help you track fitness progress. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Trend Fit App does not use any third-party services to process or store HealthKit data. All HealthKit data remains on your device and is used only for in-app functionality.
