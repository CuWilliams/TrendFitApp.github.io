Try TrendFit early through Apple TestFlight. Install the app, explore features, and send feedback directly via TestFlight.

How to Join

  • 📱 Install the free TestFlight app from the App Store.
  • 🔗 Open the public TestFlight invite link below on your iPhone or iPad.
  • ⬇️ Tap “Accept” in TestFlight to download the TrendFit beta build.
  • 🆕 New builds will appear automatically in TestFlight; update just like App Store apps.
  • 💬 Submit feedback directly through the TestFlight app (shake device or use “Send Beta Feedback”).

How is my data handled?

TestFlight feedback (including screenshots or crash logs) is managed by Apple. TrendFit never uploads your Health data — analysis remains private and on-device. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
