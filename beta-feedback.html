What to include

  • Device & iOS: e.g., iPhone 14, iOS 18.6
  • TrendFit build (if shown): e.g., 0.9.3 (123)
  • Area of the app: TrendFit, TrendFitStack, Settings, etc.
  • Steps to reproduce: numbered steps work best
  • Expected vs. actual: what you thought would happen vs. what happened
  • Screenshots or short video: attach if helpful (supported by the form)

Tip: submit one issue per entry so we can track and fix faster.
